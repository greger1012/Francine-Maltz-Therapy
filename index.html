Impartial, thorough evaluations to assist courts in determining the best interests of children

Trusted Expertise in Family Court

Child custody matters can be complex and emotionally charged. When the court needs an impartial professional to evaluate the family situation and provide recommendations in the best interests of the child, a qualified Child Custody Evaluator brings clarity and expertise to the process.

I have served as a supervisor for Child Protective Services, testifying in high-profile contested cases in Juvenile Dependency court, and directed Child Custody Recommending Counselors for San Bernardino County. My evaluations are conducted with integrity, objectivity, and a deep understanding of family dynamics, trauma, and child development.

Services

Child Custody Evaluations

Comprehensive evaluations conducted in accordance with California Family Code 3111 and court requirements. Thorough assessment of both parents, the child(ren), and family dynamics.

730 Evaluations

Areas of Specialization

Domestic Violence

40-hour DV certified training. Skilled in assessing power and control dynamics and their impact on custody decisions.

Trauma-Informed Assessment

Extensive training in trauma and its effects on children and families. Forensic interviewing expertise in child sexual abuse cases.

High-Conflict Custody

Experience with contested cases, parental alienation concerns, and complex family dynamics.

Child Development

Background in child development and parent-child relationships. Trained in Parent-Child Interaction Therapy (PCIT).

My Approach

My evaluations are conducted with objectivity, thoroughness, and respect for all parties involved. I draw on over three decades of experience in family court services, child welfare, and clinical work to provide the court with balanced, well-supported recommendations.

I maintain membership in the Association of Family and Conciliation Courts (AFCC), the Judicial Council of California, and the California Association of Marriage and Family Therapy (CAMFT)—staying current with best practices and legal standards for child custody evaluation.
